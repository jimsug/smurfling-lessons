The **ITO transmuters** are an unusual pair of mods. They don't make a portal tougher or faster to hack. Instead, they change **what kind of items it gives you** when hacked. They're sponsored mods (the name comes from the ITO EN tea brand) and they come in two flavours.

## The two types

- **ITO-** biases a portal's drops toward **offensive** gear: XMP Bursters and Ultra Strikes.
- **ITO+** biases drops toward **defensive** gear: resonators and shields.

So if you're stocking up to go wrecking, you want ITO- portals. If you're loading up to build and defend, ITO+.

## How they behave

A few rules worth knowing:

- **One is enough.** Putting two of the same type on a portal does nothing extra.
- **Opposites cancel.** An ITO- and an ITO+ on the same portal cancel each other out, leaving the portal balanced again.
- They're **Very Rare**, so you won't be swimming in them.

## Where they fit

ITO transmuters are a **farming refinement** rather than something you'll use day to day. When a team sets up a serious farm, they might transmute it one way or the other so everyone walks away with the specific gear they need for an upcoming operation. As a new agent you're more likely to **benefit** from a transmuted farm than to be setting one up yourself.

If you do find one, hang onto it. They're scarce, and an ITO- on a good farm right before a big attack op is exactly the sort of thing that makes a team's gear go further.
