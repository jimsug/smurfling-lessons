To turn a green portal blue, you first have to knock it down: destroy its resonators until it goes neutral, then capture it. That's what weapons are for. There are two, and they do different jobs.

{/* VISUAL: XMP blast radius centred on the agent, damage falling off toward the edge of the circle */}

## XMP Bursters

The **XMP Burster** (Exotic Matter Pulse) is your main weapon and the one you'll fire most. When you set one off, it detonates in a circle **centred on you**, damaging every enemy resonator inside the blast. The radius grows with the burster's level, so a level <Lvl n={8} /> burster hits a much wider area than a level <Lvl n={1} />.

The key thing about damage: it **falls off sharply with distance**. A resonator right under the blast takes full damage; one near the edge of the radius takes a fraction of it. So to bring a portal down efficiently, **get close and move around** to hit each resonator hard, rather than standing in one spot and chipping at the far ones.

## Ultra Strikes

The **Ultra Strike** is a focused blast with a very small radius, right under your feet. It does more concentrated damage in that tiny area, which makes it the tool for two jobs:

- **Stripping mods**, especially shields. A well-shielded portal shrugs off bursters until you peel the shields off, and Ultra Strikes are how you do that.
- **Finishing stubborn resonators** when you can stand right on top of them.

Stand directly on the portal to use one well.

## Firing them

Select a weapon and fire, or hold a finger on the map and swipe up to bring up the firing panel. A couple of things worth knowing:

- **You can only fire weapons at or below your own level**, the same as resonators. Higher-level gear sits locked until you level up.
- **Charge for extra damage.** Hold the fire button and a ring closes in toward your position. Release as it gets tight and you deal up to **+20% extra damage** with a better critical-hit chance. It costs nothing but a moment, so get into the habit.
- **Firing costs XM**, scaling with the weapon's level.

## Portals fight back

When you damage an enemy portal's resonators and you're close enough, the portal **zaps you**, draining your XM. Higher-level portals zap harder and from further away, and certain mods make it worse: **force amps** boost the damage, **turrets** boost how often it zaps. If your XM hits zero mid-attack, you're stuck, so keep <Term id="power-cube">power cubes</Term> handy to refill and keep firing.

## As a newer agent

Until you're level 6 or so with access to higher-level bursters, most well-built enemy portals will be tough to crack alone, and that's fine. Look for **soft targets**: portals with only low-level resonators, or ones already worn down. And remember Machina (red) portals are deliberately easy, so they're great practice and good gear. The heavy lifting on fortified enemy portals is usually a team effort, which is exactly how it's meant to work.
