On the first Saturday of every month, agents gather in cities all over the world for **Ingress First Saturday**, usually shortened to **IngressFS** or just **FS**. If there is one near you, go to it. It is the single best thing a new agent can do.

## What it actually is

FS is a **cross-faction** event, meaning both Resistance and Enlightened turn up and play together. It is organised and run by local agents, not by the company, and every city does its own slightly different version. The core of it is the same everywhere: agents meet at a set location, play for a couple of hours, and hang around afterward.

A typical FS runs something like this:

- **Check-in** at the start. You meet a point of contact, often one from each faction, and they record your starting stats.
- **An AP counting window**, usually around two hours, where everyone plays and racks up as much AP as they can. To qualify for the stats competition you generally need to earn at least **10,000 AP** during the window, which is very achievable even for a new agent.
- **A passcode** you help decode by hacking specific portals around the area. Do not share it outside the event.
- **A restocking portal** that gets boosted during the event so everyone can refill their gear.
- **A group photo**, which is part of how the event is verified.
- **Hanging about afterward.** This is the actual point. Coffee, food, chatting, meeting the people you will be playing alongside.

Show up in person and you earn the **IngressFS badge**, which tiers up the more events you attend.

## Why a brand-new agent should go

It can feel intimidating to rock up to an event when you barely understand the game. Do it anyway. FS is built for exactly this. You will be surrounded by experienced agents who will tank portals for you, hand you gear, answer every dumb question you have, and generally make sure you leave knowing far more than you arrived with. A single FS will teach you more than hours of reading.

It is also the easiest way to put faces to the names in your local chat, and to stop being "a new agent" and start being "one of ours."

## How to find one

Local FS events are listed and registered through Fev Games. Search for "IngressFS" and find the event map, or just ask in your local chat whether there is one near you and where it meets. If your area runs a strong, consistent FS, your community will already be talking about it. Get the date in your calendar and turn up.
