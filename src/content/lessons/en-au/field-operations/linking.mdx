Once you can capture and build portals, the next step is connecting them. A **link** is a beam between two of your portals, and links are how you start claiming territory for the Resistance.

## What you need to link

To create a link you need a few things lined up:

- You must be **in range of the origin portal**, the one you're linking from.
- **Both portals must be yours** (Resistance) and **fully resonated** with all eight <Term id="resonator">resonators</Term>.
- You need the **key** of the destination portal, which you get by hacking it. You can't link to a portal you don't have a key for.

The link runs from the portal you're standing at to the portal you hold the key for. So the usual flow is: hack the far portal to grab its key, travel to the near portal, then link across to the far one using that key.

## The rules that will trip you up

A few hard limits cause most "why won't it link?" moments:

- **Links can't cross other links.** Not yours, not the enemy's. If a link already crosses your intended path, you're blocked. This is the single most common reason a link fails.
- **Linking under a field is limited, not banned.** You can link out from beneath an existing <Term id="control-field">control field</Term>, but only for links shorter than **2,000 metres**. Anything longer is blocked while the field is up. (Older guides say under-field linking is impossible. It used to be capped at 500 metres, raised to 2,000 in 2024, never a flat ban.)
- **A portal has a maximum of 8 outbound links.** Only SoftBank Ultra Link mods lift that cap. The link amps in the next point change link distance, not the number of links.
- **The destination has to be within range.** Link distance is set by the origin portal's level and any link amps on it. Higher-level portals reach further.

## Why bother

Links earn AP on their own (313 each), but their real purpose is what comes next. Link three of your portals together into a triangle and you create a **control field**, which is where the actual territory game lives. That's the next lesson.

For now, the habit to build is collecting keys as you hack. Every key is a future link, and a stocked key inventory is what lets you field an area the moment it's yours.
