The **Kinetic Capsule** is one of the better ideas in the game: it lets you **craft gear by walking**. You feed it some items and XM, pick what you want to make, and then walk a set distance to forge it. Niantic's whole thing is getting you outside, and this rewards exactly that.

{/* VISUAL: a kinetic capsule Program running, with its distance progress meter filling as you walk */}

## How it works

A kinetic capsule runs **Programs**, which are recipes. Each Program takes:

- **Some input items** (which are consumed), for example a few Rare Heat Sinks and some resonators.
- **An amount of XM.**
- **Distance walked**, normally **8km** per Program.

You select a Program, tap run, and the inputs are immediately consumed. Then a progress meter fills as you walk. When you've covered the distance, you get a comm alert and claim your crafted item. You can claim it even if your inventory is over the cap, so the walk is never wasted.

If you turn on **Adventure Sync** in the settings, your walking counts even when the scanner is closed, so your everyday steps quietly chip away at a Program in your pocket.

## Getting one

- The **Common Kinetic Capsule** is free. Any agent level 4 or higher can claim one from the store, and it's **indestructible and reusable**. Get it as soon as you qualify.
- The **Rare Kinetic Capsule** works the same but is **destroyed after three uses**. You can hack these from portals at any level, or get them through C.O.R.E. and the store.

You can have up to **seven** kinetic capsules forging at the same time, and there's a daily limit of **40km** of walking that counts toward forging (anything beyond still counts toward your Trekker medal). In practice that's around five Programs a day, more during events that lower the distance requirement.

## What it's good for

The Programs let you craft genuinely useful **Very Rare** gear, things like Very Rare shields, heat sinks, multi-hacks, and SoftBank Ultra Links, plus higher-level resonators and other items, from cheaper inputs. For a newer agent, the free Common Kinetic Capsule is one of the best things you can be running in the background. Pick a Program, start it, and just play. The walking you'd do anyway turns into gear you'd otherwise struggle to find.

It's the closest the game gets to rewarding you simply for being out and moving, which, when you think about it, is the whole point of Ingress.
