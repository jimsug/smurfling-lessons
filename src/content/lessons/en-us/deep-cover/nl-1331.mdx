**NL-1331** is one of the more unusual things in Ingress, and one you'll only run into occasionally. At its heart it's the **XM Detection Vehicle**, a kitted-out van that, in the lore, roams the world monitoring exotic matter. In practice it's a roadshow and a community gathering rolled into one.

{/* VISUAL: the NL-1331 XM Detection Vehicle van */}

## The physical version

Historically, the NL-1331 van **tours** and visits communities, often around anomalies or other big events. When it comes to a town, agents gather to meet up, hack a **special NL-1331 portal** associated with the vehicle, and spend time with each other and sometimes with Niantic staff. There's a dedicated **NL-1331 Meetups medal** for attending.

These physical visits are special occasions rather than regular fixtures, and they've become rarer. Don't expect one to roll through every month. But if one is announced near you, it's a fun and fairly unique reason to gather with your local team.

## The virtual version

More recently, NL-1331 has also taken a **virtual** form, run through the Dispatch system (covered in the next lesson). In a Virtual NL Tour, communities effectively **summon** a virtual NL-1331 portal to their area by rallying online (posting photos of their local community alongside a recognizable portal, with the right hashtags), and there are Dispatch Campaigns and a commemorative medal tied to the event.

The virtual format means an NL-1331 moment can reach areas the physical van never could, which suits a smaller team running a global game.

## The short version

NL-1331 isn't something you need to chase or plan around. It's an occasional bit of flavor: sometimes a van that visits, sometimes a virtual tour you can rally your community behind. If one turns up in either form, it's a nice excuse to gather and grab a medal. If it doesn't, you're not missing anything essential. Just know what the red-and-white van is if someone mentions it.
