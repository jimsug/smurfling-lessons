Sooner or later you will see **red** on your map. Red portals, red links spreading across the area like a rash. This is **Machina**, and it is worth knowing what it is so it does not confuse you.

{/* VISUAL: red Machina portals and links spreading across the map */}

## What it is

Machina is a third faction, but unlike the Resistance and the Enlightened it is not human. It is controlled by the game itself, an AI that appeared in late 2022. Its portals and links are red, and it does not play by quite the same rules you do.

Machina spreads on its own. It seeds portals out of neutral ones and throws red links to nearby portals, turning them red too. Left alone in a quiet area, it can spread quite a bit. You do not need to understand the exact spawning rules as a new agent. You just need to know that red is the machine, and clearing it is a good thing.

## Why you can treat it as free points

Here is the good news: Machina portals are generally **easy to take down**. They carry no shields, and their resonators sit at low charge, so a handful of XMP bursters will usually neutralize one. Compared to a well-defended enemy portal, red is soft.

When you destroy a Machina portal, it does not just vanish. It **drops a pile of items on the ground**, resonators, keys, bursters, and the occasional mod, which you collect by standing within range. So clearing red is both useful to your team and a decent way to top up your gear.

Two things to know:

- **Do not bother hacking a red portal the normal way.** Hacking a Machina portal drains your entire XM bar and only ever gives level 1 gear. Destroy it instead, then pick up what it drops.
- **Capturing** a cleared Machina portal, deploying on it to turn it blue, counts toward the **Reclaimer** medal. Knocking red down helps your area either way, but it is reclaiming the portal that earns the credit.

## The short version

Red is the machine. It spreads if nobody fights it. It is easy to kill, it drops gear when it dies, and clearing it helps your area. As a new agent you do not need a strategy for it. If you see red and you have a few bursters, have a go. You will be doing your team a favor.
