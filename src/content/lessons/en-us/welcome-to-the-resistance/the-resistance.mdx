You are Resistance now. Blue. Here is what that means, in lore and in practice.

## The lore version

In the story of Ingress, XM is a powerful and poorly understood force. The **Enlightened** (green) believe XM is the next step for humanity and want to embrace it. The **Resistance** (blue) are not convinced. We treat XM with caution and work to protect people from being controlled by forces they do not understand.

If you enjoy the fiction, there is a deep and genuinely strange ongoing narrative behind all of this involving the Shapers, the N'zeer, and a cast of characters who have been at it for over a decade. You can take or leave the story. Plenty of agents never touch it and have a great time anyway.

## The version that matters

The factions play identically. Blue and green have exactly the same items, the same mechanics, the same abilities. Choosing a side is not choosing a play style. It is choosing **who you play with and who you play against**.

You are blue, which means:

- The agents who recruited you are your team. They will tank low-level portals for you, pass you gear, and show you the ropes.
- Green portals and green fields are the opposition. Your job is to turn them blue.
- The people you meet at events from the other faction are not enemies in any real sense. Ingress rivalry is friendly. You will smash each other's portals on Saturday and share chips afterward. That is normal and it is part of the fun.

The symbol is blue. The portals you capture glow blue. When you see a sea of blue on the map in your area, that is your team holding ground, and soon enough some of it will be yours.

Resist the Shapers. Or at least resist the green portal down the road. Either works.
